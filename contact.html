ABOUT THE AUTHOR

Tomeka lives in Oakland, CA with her 12 year old daughter and their 4 year old pitbull Raya. Born and raised in the California Bay Area, she has called Oakland home for over 20 years. She is an active community participant and advocates for black and brown women in tech and minority women with criminal backgrounds, and serves as an up and coming voice for all minorities and underrepresented communities of the world.

In 2019, right before the onslaught of the COVID pandemic, Tomeka partnered with AnnieCannons, a local non-profit advocating for individuals coming out of traumatic, violent and trafficking situations by teaching them how to code and both infiltrate and navigate the tech industry.

Since learning HTML, CSS, JavaScript and React, Tomeka has not looked back and has started many creative ventures with her new found knowledge and talent, including ColorCodedDev, a design and development firm that hires women of color exclusivly giving them the chance to break into the front end development part of the tech industry and show their skills off to the world. ColorCodedDev also features a website and blog which both serve as platforms for Tomeka to share her message and knowledge with other aspiring coders, and development enthusiasts alike.

You can find, follow and contact Tomeka for professional purposes on Instagram or reach out to her at one of the other social media links above.
