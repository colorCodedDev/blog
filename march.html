Questions To Ask In An Interview

Questions About The Company

  • How would you describe the culture here?

  • What challenges is the company currently facing?

  • What type of people are successful in this company?

Questions About the Team

  • How are the responsibilities of the development team broken up?

  • How do developers on the team give feedback to each other?

  • Who will be the report line? Will I have a chance to meet with them?

Questions About the Position

  • What would a typical workday look like?

  • What do you expect from this position?

  • What kind of feedback can I get in the first few months?

Questions About the Interviwer

  • How long have you worked for this company?

  • When did you start programming? How did you learn?

  • What’s your favorite part about working here?
